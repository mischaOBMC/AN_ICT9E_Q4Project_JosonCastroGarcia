Waffle Bites




Ingredients

  • 95 g unsalted butter room temperature
  • 100 g sugar
  • pinch salt
  • 1 egg XL
  • 120 g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¬º tsp nutmeg
  • ¬Ω tsp cinnamon
  • ¬æ cup buttermilk room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • extra butter, for greasing waffle iron

Instructions

  1. Using a stand mixer, beat the butter, sugar and salt on medium for 5 minutes
  2. Meanwhile, pre-heat the waffle iron
  3. Add the egg and beat for another 2 minutes
  4. Sift the dry ingredients
  5. In a small jug, combine the buttermilk and vanilla extract
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the mixer in 3 batches, alternating with the milk and vanilla
  7. Melt extra butter and brush it lightly into the waffle iron
  8. Use a small icecream scoop to scoop out dough onto the waffle iron. I do 4 scoops at a time, which works well in my iron.
  9. Bake for 3 minutes, until lightly golden
  10. Remove from iron and cool on a cake rack. Waffles will be soft at first, but crisp up on cooling.
  11. Continue with your chosen coating
