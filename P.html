Cheeeeesy Pastina




Ingredients

  • ¬æ cup (4oz / 120g) pastina
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 tablespoons freshly grated parmesan cheese (ensure it's finely grated)
  • Drizzle of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Garnish (Optional)

  • Extra grated parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small pot, pour in the vegetable broth and season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook over a medium-high heat with the lid on and bring to a boil
  2. Once boiling, add the pastina and stir through the broth. Continue to stir it every now and then so it doesn‚Äôt clump or stick to the bottom. Cook until al dente (according to package instructions, usually 5-8 mins)
  3. Once cooked, turn off the heat and add in the finely grated parmesan cheese with a drizzle of olive oil. Continuously stir the pastina for 1-2 minutes to thicken the broth and create a creamy consistency
  4. Serve in bowls and garnish with a light drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, extra parmesan cheese and black pepper and enjoy your yummy bowl of Italian Pastina!
