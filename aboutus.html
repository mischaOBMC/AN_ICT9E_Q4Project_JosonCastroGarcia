About Us!

Lutong Bahay" is a celebration of the heart and soul of Filipino home cooking, bringing together the warmth, flavors, and traditions that define every Filipino kitchen. This cookbook is a curated collection of recipes passed down through generations, capturing the essence of home-cooked meals that remind us of family gatherings, festive celebrations, and everyday comfort. Whether you're a seasoned cook or just beginning your culinary journey, *Lutong Bahay* offers a variety of simple yet delicious recipes that make it easy to recreate the rich tastes of Filipino cuisine in your own home. With each page, we invite you to experience the joy of cooking and the love that goes into every dish, making your meals not just food, but memories shared around the table.

Our Goal

At Lutong Bahay, our goal is to inspire and empower home cooks to embrace the beauty of Filipino cuisine in their everyday lives. We strive to make traditional Filipino recipes accessible, approachable, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of skill level. By preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines, we aim to foster a deeper appreciation for our food culture while bringing families and friends closer together through the joy of cooking and sharing meals. Whether you're looking to recreate nostalgic favorites or explore new flavors, Lutong Bahay is here to help you create authentic, heartwarming dishes that bring comfort and connection to your table.

Our Vision

Our vision at Lutong Bahay is to become a cherished resource for Filipino home cooks around the world, bridging generations and cultures through the universal language of food. We envision a future where Filipino cuisine is celebrated and recognized for its diversity, richness, and warmth, inspiring a global community to connect with their roots and explore new culinary horizons.
