HOW DO I LOAD MY BEEP CARD?


To load an MRT beep card, first, visit a designated beep card loading station at an MRT station or an authorized partner store. Next, insert your beep card into the loading machine or present it to the cashier at the store. Choose the amount you want to load onto the card, either through cash or electronic payment options, and complete the transaction. After the transaction is successful, the machine or cashier will return your beep card with the new balance.
