SOUR CREAM FLAVOR

Ingredients

  1. Potato Fries
  2. Sour Cream Powder
  3. Salt
  4. Garlic Powder
  5. Onion Powder
  6. Citric Acid

Instructions

  1. Cook the fries – Fry or bake the potato fries until crispy.
  2. Season – While fries are hot, sprinkle sour cream powder mix evenly.
  3. Toss – Gently toss fries to coat them with seasoning.
  4. Serve – Enjoy immediately!
